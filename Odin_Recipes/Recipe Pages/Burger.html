Burger Recipe

Description

A burger is a popular fast-food dish made with a juicy patty, fresh vegetables, cheese, and sauces placed inside soft buns. It is filling, flavorful, and enjoyed all around the world.

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 beef or chicken patty
  • 2 cheese slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Cook the burger patty on a hot pan or grill.
  2. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  3. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on the buns.
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, and onion slices.
  5. Place the cooked patty on the bun.
  6. Add cheese slices on top of the patty.
  7. Cover with the top bun.
  8. Serve the burger while hot.
