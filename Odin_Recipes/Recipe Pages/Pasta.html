Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta is a classic Italian dish made with soft noodles and flavorful sauce. It is simple to prepare, delicious to eat, and can be served with cheese, herbs, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add salt and pasta to the boiling water.
  3. Cook the pasta for 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  5. Add garlic and cook until golden.
  6. Add tomato sauce and mix well.
  7. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce.
  8. Top with cheese and black pepper before serving.
