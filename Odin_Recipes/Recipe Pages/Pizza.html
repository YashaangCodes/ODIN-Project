Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a soft dough base, rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and delicious toppings. It is baked until the crust becomes golden and crispy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 packet yeast
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough using flour, yeast, water, and salt
  2. Let the dough rest for 1 hour
  3. Roll the dough into a circular shape
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the dough
  5. Add mozzarella cheese and toppings
  6. Sprinkle oregano on top
  7. Bake the pizza in a preheated oven at 220°C for 15 minutes
  8. Slice and serve hot
